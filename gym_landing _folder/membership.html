Membership

Join the InevitaFit GYM Community

At InevitaFit GYM, we offer a range of membership options designed to fit your lifestyle and fitness goals. Our memberships provide access to state-of-the-art facilities, expert trainers, and a supportive community that will help you stay motivated and achieve your fitness objectives

Membership Options:

Standard Membership

  • Access: All gym equipment, cardio machines, and group fitness classes.
  • Hours: Standard gym hours.
  • Benefits: Free fitness assessment and introductory session with a personal trainer.

Premium Membership

  • Access: All facilities and equipment, plus premium classes such as yoga, pilates, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT).
  • Hours: Extended hours and early access to certain facilities.
  • Benefits: Priority booking for classes, complimentary towel service, and access to our exclusive member lounge.

    How to Join

    Becoming a member is simple! Visit our gym, or contact us online to find out more about our membership plans and special offers. Our friendly staff is here to assist you with the sign-up process and answer any questions you may have.
